What it is

Devfest 2019

Devfest, a college level hackathon, aims to bring out the best of the talents in the fields of Coding, Developing and Designing. DevFest acts as a platform for the developers to showcase and present their innovative ideas through the hackathon. DevFest, a one-stop spot where excellent speakers and experts from the industry, mentor and motivate enthusiastic participants and share their past experiences with them.

Event Flow

Networking

Developers will get a chance to interact and discuss ideas with industry leading experts on their projects as well as other technical domains. This will be a hugely rewarding opportunity for those who wish to learn and expand their knowledge in the field.

The 36 - hour long hackathon will be spread across in various stages, filtering out the best teams that will compete in the final round. Participants will be encouraged to solve real-life problems by implementing the ideas given in the tracks, under the constant mentorship of industrial experts.
